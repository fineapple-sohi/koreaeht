Reliable energy-efficient switch heating systems
 
The Thermal-Flex High Capacity Switch Heating System provides a unique combination of ruggedness, unparalleled effectiveness and low power consumption.
 
The flexible braided heating cable resists the physical stresses common in railroad operations. It operates at substantially lower temperatures than those routinely encountered in stress-relieving. And its unique construction will withstand heat and electrical stresses, concurrent in all forms of industrial heating.
 
The water-tight connectors and other elements of the system are designed for reliable and extended use.
 
Through the use of thermal insulation over the heating cable on the stock rails, virtually all the heat is driven into the rails. The rail thus becomes the heating agent rather than the heating element itself, as in tubular heaters. Accordingly, a typical system uses a nominal 100 watts per foot to heat the stock rails.
 
The Crib heaters supply an additional 300 watts per foot at the point-of-switch and around the rod area, where the elimination of ice and snow is most critical.
 
Since no drilling of the rails is required, and since spring tensioned steel clips hold the stock rail heating assemblies in place, the typical Thermal-Flex system can be installed in about one to two hours, and can be isassembled in about twenty minutes.
