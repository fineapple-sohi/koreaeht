CONSTRUCTION
 
A. HEATER:
 ㆍHeater Buss Wires #10 AWG (37/26) Nickel Plated Copper.
 ㆍBuss wire insulation PFA Teflon extruded jacket bound together with PFA Teflon extruded in a figure-8 design.
 ㆍNickel Chrome (A) heating element spirally wrapped and soldered at specific zone lengths and covered with PFA Teflon.
 ㆍA Nickel Plated Copper over-braid shield, 80% minimum coverage is applied for additional protection and grounding.
 ㆍA final over-jacket of PFA Teflon is applied overall. Nominal finish OD: .440" X .315".
 
B. MARKING AND IDENTIFICATION:
Identification will be continuously visible as raised lettering formed into the flat section of the heater
cable final over-jacket.
 ㆍManufacturer's Name
 ㆍVoltage rating
 ㆍWatts per foot
 ㆍTemperature rating
 ㆍType reference
 
C. TERMINATION'S:
1. Heater terminations will be made at Thermal-Flex facility to a determined length as specified
by customer. Termination lead lengths will also be specified.
2. Terminations can also be made in the field. Proper instructions and materials will be provided
if this option is required.
 
D. CONTAINMENT CHANNEL:
1. Heater channel will be manufactured of Politen ESP220 (UVL protected) Fiberglass and
formed to fit over third rail heater in a snug manner as not to incur excess pressure which may
damage heater.
2. Channel will be supplied in 6 foot lengths measuring 1.000" X 0.438 dimensions. The channel
will be held in place with spring steel zinc plated rail clips, spaced at approximately 18" inches.
 
E. SPRING STEEL RAIL CLIP:
1. Spring steel clip for securing stainless steel channel against rail for maintaining direct contact
of third rail heater with rail. Clip will be manufactured of (0.075" X 1.50") 14 gauge annealed
spring steel - C1050. Spring steel clips will be heat treated & plated with zinc chromate
finish for weather protection.
