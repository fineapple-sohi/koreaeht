GENERAL OVERVIEW
 
A. System specification outline includes design and materials utilized in the
manufacturing, testing and installation of third rail de-icing & snow melting. Cable
is designed specifically for third rail de-icing & snow melting.
 
B. Heater characteristics include
 ㆍParallel construction design
 ㆍSolid Nickel Chrome A element wire
 ㆍPFA Teflon insulation
 ㆍ#10 Conductors & protective braid will be Nickel plated
 
C. Cable output and temperature under load rated 600 VDC 30 to 40 watts
a foot. Maximum temperature rating of 250°C.
 
D. Heater Cable can be removed or reinstalled when de-energized and
cooled.
 
E. Heater Cable is compatible with most control panels for
transit company third rail de-icing & snow melting.
