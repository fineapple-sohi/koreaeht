Leak Detection Sys. - LD5000


The LD5000 detects and reports the presence and exact location of water and other conductive liquids. When a conductive liquid comes in contact with RLE's patented leak detection cable, the system pinpoints the location of the leak. Within seconds, the system's alarm sounds and the distance to the leak is shown on the four line liquid crystal display (LCD).
The operator then cross-reference this distance with the cable route map and quickly locates the leak.

The LD5000 works in concert with RLE's patented leak detection cable. The unit can monitor up to 5000 feet of leak detection cable. The unique design of the LD5000 eliminates high humidity false		 모 alarms that can occur with other systems.

The LD5000 is manufactured in a metal surface mount enclosure. An end terminator is provided with the LD5000.  The device operates on 120VAC. An internal power supply is included.
