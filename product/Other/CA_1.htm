Cathodic Protection Sys.


Steel-in-Concrete-CPBD
Matcor remains the consistent leader in the development of steel-in-concrete cathodic protection systems for highway bridges, buildings, and parking structures.
In addition to the development of many of the theories applied today, Matcor offers equipment designed specifically for steel-in-concrete cathodic protection: rectifier/controllers, continuous platinum anodes, corrosion rate probes, grout and reference cells.
Matcor provides design, components and construction management.


For more information regarding Cathodic Protection, consult Matcor Technical Bulletin : D-1184

Custom Designed Systems
Matcor engineers will evaluate, analyze, and design cathodic protection systems for unique application. Matcor is the manufacturer of many of the proprietary products in use today.
We have the experience to provide systems particularly suited for each application. Custom systems have ranged from providing D.C. current to 16th Century Spanish cannons to creating platinum anodes for pasteurizers. Contact Matcor to discuss your special application.
