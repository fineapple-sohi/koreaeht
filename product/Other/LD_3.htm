Leak Detection Sys. - SC


 RLE's patented(PAT.6,144,209) leak detection cable reliably senses and signals water and conductive fluids.
RLE's cable is easy to install, resists bends and kinks, and lies flat after installation.
The plenum rates, UL listed cable can withstand temperatures up to 176℉.
The cables terminate with mating connectors; installation  and replacement is quick and easy. Cables are sold in premeasured lengths of 25, 50 and 100 feet. The cables can be connected end to end when extra length is needed. Custom lengths can be provided, and a modular cable design allows existing leak detection to be expanded in the field.

An abrasion resistant polymer core increases the strength of the cable and properly separates the sensing wires from each other. The cable dries quickly without the use of heaters or drying devices.		 　 The cable's flexibility provides for a small bend radii. No conductive polymers are used in the cable's construction. This eliminates shorts that may result from metal, such as spiral wrap conduit or raise floor pedestals, rubbing  against the cable.


This cable's two sensing wires and two return wires are configured to determine not only the presence of a fluid, but also its exact location on the floor. RLE's cable resists damage from most contaminates. The cable is compatible with the LDZ series and the LD5000.
