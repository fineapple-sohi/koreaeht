 The LDZ1 (one zone), LDZ4 (four zone) , and LDZ8 (eight zone) zone leak detection systems detect and report the presence of water and any other conductive liquids.
The LDZ1 monitors up to 150 feet of RLE's patented leak detection cable. The LDZ4 and LDZ8 monitor up to 1000 feet of leak detection cable per zone. When a leak is detected, the LDZ control panel visibly and audibly annunciates the leak and in which zone the leak is located. The LDZs are supervised systems -  they monitors the cable for breaks and misconnections. Summary alarm relay output , cable trouble alert, and sensitivity adjustments for each zone are standard. End terminators are provided.
　		 　 The LDZ1 is manufactured in a plastic enclosure. The LDZ4 is manufactured in a metal enclosure. Both units operate on 24VAC power. Power supplies are not included with either unit, but can be purchased separately.

The LDZ8 features a variety of enhancements. It is constructed in a metal enclosure and operates on an internal 120 VAC power supply (provide). The LDZ8 interfaces with a computer or terminal through a standard EIA - 232 port. The last 100 alarms can be viewed for analysis, and the unit stores a history of the cable's current leakage.
