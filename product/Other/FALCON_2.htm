Web Camera

A live picture of a critical or remote site lets users see who is at their facility and what events are taking place there. Integrating a web camera into the Falcon provides this live image. The web camera's output is viewed on the same Falcon-hosted web page as the parameters the Falcon monitors.
Users have access to their live image and site information  on one screen.  The Falcon and the web camera connect via a local network;  the camera may be as close to or as far away from the Falcon as necessary.


Temperature / humidity
Sensor
