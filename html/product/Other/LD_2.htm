Spot Detector
Spot detectors (SDs) use polymercoated sensing probes to detect conductive fluids at a single point.
SDs are the most economical way to detect water and are commonly used in air conditioning drip pans, small rooms, and around floor drains.
¡¡

¡¡
Each SD must connect to a control module. RLE control modules are available for one (RASP1), six (RASP6), or twelve (RASP12) SDs.

Water Seeker 50
The Water Seeker 50 (WS50) is a non-supervised single zone leak detection system. The WS50 accepts an input form RLE's leak detection cable or a single point sense bracket and provides a dry contact output. No audible  or		 ¡¡ visible annunciation is provided.



The unit can accommodate up to 200 feet of sensing cable.  WS50s are frequently used with air conditioners that have leak detection systems associated with their controllers. They can also be placed inside air conditioners are powered by any 24VAC/VDC source.
