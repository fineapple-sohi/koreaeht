Leak Detection Sys.


RLE  Technologies manufactures three different  types of leak detection systems:spot detectors,zone leak detection systems, and distance-read leak detection systems. Each system offers its own unique benefits.


Spot Detector
A spot detector senses  water at a single point. They are most frequently used in small rooms, air conditioning drip pans or dams, and around floor drains. Spot detectors are the most economical way to detect water.


Each spot detector must connect to a control module. This control module is usually mounted on a wall in a high traffic area and provides visible and audible annunciation of a water leak. A typical spot detector application includes one or multiple spot detectors connected to RLE's RASP unit.


Cable System
Cable systems are suggested for large areas. They provide better floor coverage than spot detectors. Cable Systems can be configured as zone systems or distance-read systems.


Zone System
When a zone systems is installed, sensing cable is placed on the floor or sub-floor around leak sources.
The space is divided into multiple area, or zones. Each cable monitors one zones. If water or other conductive liquids contact the cable anywhere along it slength, the control panel annunciates the leak and in which zone the leak is located. Control panels are available to support one(LDZ1), four(LDZ4), or eight zone's(LDZ8).

 　

Each individual zone's sensitivity is adjustable.


Distance-Read System
Distance-read systems pinpoint the location of a water leak. Distance-read systems are particularly useful in facilities with large raised-floor area.



This technology features up to 5000 feet of leak detection cable. The cable is placed on the floor or sub-floor around possible leak sources. When a leak occurs, the control head annunciates this information and provides a distance measurement. This distance measurement is cross-referenced with the cable route map and the location of the leak is calculated within a few feet.


An LD5000 control head is used with the distance-read system. Sensitivity is adjustable.
