Falcon Monitoring Sys.


Falcon


The Falcon can monitor a variety of parameters and equipment, including:


• Temperature

• Humidity

• DC power plant alarms

• Commercial power on / off

• Fire suppression status

• Leak detection systems

• Battery monitoring system

• UPS

• Generator

• Precision HVAC

• ATS

• TVSS

• Access keypad

 The Falcon Monitoring System accepts up to 32 inputs, in any combination of dry contact,  4~20mA, 0~5V , or 0~10V.
