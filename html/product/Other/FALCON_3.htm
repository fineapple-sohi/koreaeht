Falcon Monitoring Sys. - Gas Sensor, Smoke Detector, Facility Access


Gas Sensor
RLE's gas sensors detect the presence of volatile vapors.
Although hydrogen detectors are the most commonly sold sensor, devices to detect a wide variety of gases are available.
Contact RLE for a complete list of gas sensors.


Gas Sensor (not pictured)
Current sensors are available in many different amperage ranges and as either solid core or split core devices. Many models provide selectable 4~20mA or dry contact outputs.
Contact RLE to find out which sensor best suits a specific application.


Smoke Detector
RLE offers a photoelectric smoke detector that is powered by the Falcon. This detector provides a dry contact input that allows it to interface directly with the Falcon.







Facility Access Tools
The Falcon accepts a keypad interface. This features allows the Falcon to controls access to a facility. RLE also offers a magnetic door sensor to complete the facility access package.
