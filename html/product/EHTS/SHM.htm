Silicone Heating Mats

Silicone heating-mats are chemically neutral, highly flexible, variable and adapted to the particular heating-problem.

The high temperature-resistance allows a variety of applications.

The effort of assembly is low and the closed surface facilitates the cleaning

Especially for higher quantities the ratio price-efficiency is optimal and  favourable  compared to other types of heating.

Our project engineers will find the individual solution in discussion with our customer.

Temperature control is provided by integrated and encapsulated power controllers and electronical  control devices, e.g. with PT-100 sensor. Moreover, capillary-tube controllers are applicable ( see eltherm - catalogue measuring and control-technique)		 모
