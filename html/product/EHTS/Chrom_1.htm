Component heaters include the basic types of Chromalox heating elements - tubular, strip, ring, disc 　 and cartridge - as well as flexible and band and nozzle heaters. There elements may, by themselves, be used to solve 　 many heating problems, or may be incorporated into other more complex heater forms.
　
Cartridge -  Chromalox cartridge heaters are available in several types.
Type CIR - High watt density hitemp (1500℉) with standard Type F
flexible leads - the best available
Type C - Medium watt density hitemp(1200℉) with Type A leads.		 　
　
Rings & Discs - The same rugged construction as a Chromalox strip heater, only made as a ring. Ideal for clamping to or mounting on round surface and small containcers.

　		 　 Chromalox Strip Heaters - are the original industrial heating
element enclosed in a metal sheath. Still the most rugged,
durable, long lasting element available to industry, it is easy to
apply and can solve virtually any heating problem.
Flexible heaters are made to order in a wide variety of shapes and sizes to satisfy your exact heating requirements 　
　

　		 　
　
Band & Nozzle Heaters - For plastic injection molding machines and any cylindrical object such as autoclaves, pressure cookers, flanges, pipes, etc. 　
　 　
Chromalox industrial hot plates are especially designed
for efficient, continuous,  heavy-duty laboratory work.
　

　
Chromalox Tubular Elements - are a versatile heating component. They can be easily formed into almost any shape to apply heat to the exact spot where heat is required.
