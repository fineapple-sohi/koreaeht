Heating Mats

Flexible heating mats are the optimal type of heating with high efficiency and good heat-distribution.
Heating mats are mainly manu-factured for individual applications, of various materials, for most different operation temperatures and according to the requirements of our customers. Tailor made segments ensure a simple and quick installation.
Suitable for containers ,hoppers, tanks, valves, blowers etc.
Designed for temperatures up to 750℃., also with ex-protection or complete with thermal insulation upon request.
