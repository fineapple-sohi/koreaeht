Rail Heating

The application

The eltherm EL-RAIL is designed to meet the special requirement of rail frost protection heating:
• high voltage
• high output, sufficient for single tracing of each
• no voltage drop
• negligible inrush current
• superior ruggedness
• weather- and UV-resistance
• easy installation

The cable

The eltherm EL-RAIL is a flat cable, combining six PTFE-insulated series heating conductors (optional with nickeled  copper braid) in a silicone overjacket.

　		 　

Technical Data:

Max. loading..............150 W/m
Max. voltage..............750 V
Max. current...............20 A per conductor
Max. circuit length at 150 W/m
    ..........400 m / 750 V or 125 m / 230 V
          (supply from both sides)
          200 m / 750 V or 62.5 m / 230 V
          (supply from one side)
Dimensions...............6 x 27 mm
Weight......................approx. 400 g/m
