The control cabinets type ELHKV are designed for 1 or 2 single-phrase heating circuits or for maximum 4 three-phrase heating circuit with self regulating heating tapes.

A main switch turns the heating circuits ON or OFF. Each heating circuit is protected separately by a circuit breaker. Terminals for an external controller, for heating tape and for a failure signal are provided for each heating circuit.

In the AUTOMATIC position, the heating tapes are switched ON or OFF by an external themostat.

The cabinets are equipped with 3 LEDs :

• Green : Power ON

• Yellow : Heating ON

• Red : Failure
