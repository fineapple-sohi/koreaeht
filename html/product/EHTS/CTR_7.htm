Power components

The Chromalox line of SCR power controllers covers the full range of power and heat control applications - simply, economically, and reliably, A large number of manufactures use Chromalox power components as original equipment controllers work without mechanical, moving parts and are capable of millions of reliable operations. And because the SCR's fast ON/OFF cycling maintains process temperature much closer to set point, heater life is extended by elimination of thermal shock. Whatever the application, you can depend on Chromalox for the ultimate degree of power control.

Model 4115 Solid State Power Controller
Featuring dual SCR design, the Model 4115 accepts any DC control input signal from 3 to 32V DC, for ON/OFF switching of 40 ro 90A, 120 to 480V AC loads.

Series 4117 AC Power Controller
Engineered for fast, easy installation and long-term dependability, the 4117 is a complete zero cross-over- fired, 3-phase, 2-leg power controller. The 4117 will accept either 4-20mA control signal input or 8 to 48V DC voltage input. Multiple units can be powered by the same control signal.  Loads up to 20kW for 240V AC and 59 kW for 480V AC can be switched by the 4117.

Series 4120 SCR Power Controller
A totally new concept in packaging for SCR power instrumentation that results in space savings and more efficient heat dissipation! The zero crossover-fired,3 phase, 2-leg design and easy application of l²T fusing and power termination, combined with unique packaging, offer OEM's the most advanced power control system available. The 240V AC units are rated up to 89kW and 480V AC units are rated up to 170kW.
