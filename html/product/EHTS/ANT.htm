Antenna Heating

The formation of ice and snowfall on antennas disturbs the trouble-free reception and the operation, especially on parabolic-aerials. In the commercial operation of antennas these failures will cause high costs.
KoreaEHT-heatings reliably prevent the formation of snow and ice and keep the operation of the antenas trouble-free.
We have successfully applied more than 1000 heating-systems during the last years for our customers all over Germany.
KoreaEHT-antenna-heatings are designed, supplied and installed as completed shell-systems for larger quantities or are individually designed for smaller quantites or for single-part production.
Please contact our project-engineers.

Technical Data :

  • contructionally adapted heating-shells of Styrosun for antennas of approx. 0.9 - 5 m diameter.
    Normally the shells are fixed with special clamping systems.
  • PTFE-cable
  • Parallel heating tapes
  • Selfregulating heating tapes
  • Heating rods
  • Heating mats
  • Air heaters

Depending on the technical requirements, all types of heating can be designed either for the whole diameter or only for the lower half.

Control :
Capillary-tube controllers/ electronic controllers/air-thermostats or electronic control type ELSAT-1 or ELSAT-2 with the recording of all important temperature data and the supervision of heating systems.
Power :
Approx. 500-600 W/㎡ or 400 Volt
Temperatures :
-30 to +70℃
Documentation:
by KoreaEHT project engineers
Installation :
through KoreaEHT or the customer according to detailed installation instructions

For larger antennas of more than 5 mdia. we will in any case offer individual solutions, incl. KoreaEHT installation or supervision of the installation on site.
