Heat Chambers

Electro-Heat-Chambers stand out for their extremely high efficiency in heating-up processes.
Their range of application is manifold and goes far beyond the traditional heating of drums or small containers.

The KoreaEHT heat-chambers can also be designed for the heating of solid bodies.The particular requirements of our customers are taken into account by e.g.change of power, different equipment or special dimensions.

Heating
By electro-flatpressed heating elements, heating-cables or heating-mats. They are arranged at the side or in the rearwall of the chamber (resistance heating), situated favourably to the flow in the air ducts, standard connection-voltage 230/400 Volt.

Control
Electronical, microprocessor-operated controllers for the temperature-control, for high accuracy with a three-figure LED-indication.

Further characteristics, as basic operation of the controller, the monitoring-operation and the control-characteristics will be adjusted-depending on the requirements- by eltherm. Temperature-limitors are also provided to avoid overheating.

Design
Sheet-metal design with square-section tube. Inner and outer sheets galvanized, complete chamber varnished, colour as requested, ther-mal insulation 80 mm.

 

Air Circulation
Fans are provided for the air circulation to guarantee a gentle heating-up of the medium in the drums.

De-Aeration
In case of occuring vapours or odours an exhaust nozzle with a throttle valve is provided for the connection of an exhaust-line
