Drum Heating

Drum heaters of the type series ELFH are designed for the heating of standing 200 liter drums.
The robust construction (inner and outer steel jacket) avoids a damage of the heating even in case of frequently exchanging the drum. The three rollers facilitate the manoeurvring of the heating around the drum.
The installed temperature control is infinitely variable from 50 to 300℃.
Other temperature ranges are available upon request.

The additional use of a bottom heater type ELBO-1, 5 is recommended for heating-up processes and to increase the efficiency.
An insulating-lid type EL-ID should be mounted onto the drum-heater to reduce the heat losses on top.
Our delivery-programme also includes drum-heaters for lying drums, consisting of a bottom-part(tipping device) and an upper part.
Some of the heating systems are also available in ex-design.

Electronic controllers with LED-indication can be applied to optimize the temperature-control.
