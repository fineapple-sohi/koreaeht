Flatpressed Tubular Heating Elements


KoreaEHT- flatpressed tubular heating elements are seamless, highly-compressed heating rods of two- or three-wire switching, with a one or two-sided connection. The heating coil consists of a nickel/ chromium alloy, and the insulation material is pure magnesium oxide.
With a maximum length of 6000 mm, you can choose between two dimensions:
Type B: 16.5 x 6.5 mm, two- or three-wire switching or Type S: 13 x 5.5 mm, two-wire switching.
The outer sheath is stainless steel 1.4571, on principle.
For special cases, however, copper, stainless steel 1.4435, Monell, Incoloy 800, Inconel 600, titanium or PTFE-coatings are available.
