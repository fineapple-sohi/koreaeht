TPK - Silicone Sealant
This option is used to seal both ends of the tubing bundle from moisture. It is a black silicone RTV sealant. Cure time is approximately 24 hours at 77℉(25℃). Service temperature ranges from -50℉(-45℃) to 400℉(205℃). TPKSK offers excellent resistance to weather, oil and many chemiclas.

  

TPKES - Heat Shrink Entry  Seal
The heat-shrinkable entry  seal provides  waterproof fitting where TRACEPAK enters an enclosure. They can be added to parting line or surface mounted plates on VIPAK enclosure or any enclosure with up a  1/2 " (13mm) wall.
The thermally stabilized, modified polyolefin entry seal consists of an O-ring assembly that seals at the enclosure and a heat-shrinkable nose that seals to the TRACEPAK bundle.


TPKHS - Heat Shrink Boots
The heat-shrinkable boots provide a weatherproof end seal for TRACKPAK tubing bundle. They are made of thermally stabilized, modified polyolefin. Using a heat shrink seal boot is recommended for all exposed ends. This installation will provide the best weather seal protection, The silicone end seal alone may be used to seal the end of the bundle inside of a VIPAK endlosure.



TPKJL - Jacket Pathch
The jacket patch kits are used to seal a splice in a bundle or to extend the insulation and weatherproof jacket should the bundle be cut back too far during installation. They are used as a repair patch kit is required with the optional line temperature sensing thermostat. Each kit contains thermal insulation, fiberglass tape and a self-sealing patch.
