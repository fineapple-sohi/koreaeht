ThermTrace FloorHeat is a self-regulating heating
tape that may be used for freeze protection, or
low temperature maintenance for floor heating.		 Maximum temperature                       65뫌
Nominal voltage                             230V
Minimum installation temperature           -30뫌
Minimum bending radius                   35mm

Self-regulating heating tapes consist of two parallel
buswires, embedded semi-conductive self-regulating
matrix. This means that the heating cable
automatically responds to changes in ambient
conditions.		 Rated output in concrete at 25뫉C           15 W/m
