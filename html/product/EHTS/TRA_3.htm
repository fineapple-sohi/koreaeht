TPH is recommended for use on impulse lines for instrumentation, process lines for analyzers, sampling, additives and other small process lines where higher temperature maintenance is necessary. It is especially  important for viscosity control.

Heavy tracing keeps the process tubing in direct contact with the tracer and maintains higher process temperatures.
