HEATED HOSES FOR THE MEDIUM-PRESSURE RANGE
Type series ELH / md

Application Range
Heating-up, temperature maintenance and transport of media in :

cold-and hotmelt-mechines, labeling machines, dosing-or paint-spraying equipment, spraying-equipment for double-glass windows, food-industry, oil-burner leads.
