Gas Cylinder Heaters

Gas-cylinder heaters are applied for the temperature maintenance of gases, e.g. CO2(approx. 40℃). This guarantees a 100% emptying and utilization of the cylinders.
Gas-cylinder heaters are subject to the pressure-gas regulations, therefore the heating is protected against over-temperature via a temperature-limitor with a blocking to reconnect.
The heatings are also suitable for an evacuation of the gas-cylinders before they are filled again. For this, operation temperatures up to 250℃ are possible. The gas-cylinders heaters are extremely robust (with inner and outer steel jacket), easily to install around the cylinders and are closed with rapid locking devices. Temperature-control and limitation are attached to one half of the heating
The cylinder-heating can also be supplied in ex-design. Special sizes and various characteristics upon inquiry.
