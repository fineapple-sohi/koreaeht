Container Heating

KoreaEHT has heated a great number of standard- and special containers during the last years.
These containers are transported on the road, by train as well as on ships.
The application temperature goes up to 200℃ and the temperature-tolerances vary - depending on the medium - from +/- 10℃ down to +/- 2℃ for extremeley temperature-sen-sitive media.
In many cases the KoreaEHT project-engineers have applied new methods with regard to the heating-material as well as to the temperature-recording and temperature-control of the container-heatings
KoreaEHT container heatings successfully operate in Europe, Canada, USA and the Far East. The container heatings are individually designed, manufactured and installed under consideration of the given technical data and the requirements of our customers.

 

 

Technical Data
Types of Heating :

  • PTFE-cable
  • mineral-insulated cable with outer metal-sheath
  • heating mats
  • selfregulating heating tapes
  • parallel heating tapes
  • flat heating rods, if necessary, also in ex -design
Control :
Capillary-tube thermostats, electronic controllers, integrated in control-boards which are adapted to the outer frame of the container.

 
  Supervision: Supervision of heating-circuit, under- or over-temperature control, data-logger with different temp. measuring-points, all systems adapted to the requirements.
Installation:
By KoreaEHT field service or the customer after instruction of an KoreaEHT project-engineers.
Inspection:
Through the customer after KoreaEHT-inspection/quality-records or through KoreaEHT project-engineers.
Documentation:
through KoreaEHT project-engineers
Temperatures:
from frost-protection up to approx.
200℃, for certain applications even higher.
Power:
variable, e.g.for 20 ft container up to 12kW/230-400V, depending on temperatures and requested heating-up periods.
