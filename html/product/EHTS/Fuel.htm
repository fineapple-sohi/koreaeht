Fuel Oil Heaters

Chromalox inline fuel oil heaters
coupled with Chromalox controls are designed to save you substantial energy costs in areas
such as liquid fuel forwarding by
providing precise heating and tight temperature controls for higher fuel burn efficiencies.
Inline heaters heat only the amount of material that is to be
pumped from storage, so the entire amount does not need heating. Electric heat provides 100 percent efficiency because
all heat produced goes into your
process. Their weather-proof
construction makes them ideal
for outdoor installation.
Enclosures are moisture - and
explosion-resistant.
