4482 Mini-Panel
For smaller load applications, the Chromalox 4482 Mini-Panel is completely wired and available off-the-shelf. Each panel includes a 3282 SS-AR controller and thermocouple.
For either 240 or 480V AC 3-phase, or 120V AC single phase, the 4482 can control 40,75 or 100 amp loads. Models are available with or without control power transformers.
