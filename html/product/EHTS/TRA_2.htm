The process tubes and tracer tubes are individually wrapped with insulation to purposely reduce heat transfer.

TPL can maintain temperatures between 50℉(10℃) and 200℉(93℃). It provides a more constant tube temperatures over a longer length than heavy traced designs.

It is suited for small diameter process lines such as those used for sampling and additives.

TPL is recommended for freeze protection of instrument impulse lines as well as the process lines for analyzers.
