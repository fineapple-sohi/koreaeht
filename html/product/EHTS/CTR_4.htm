Alarm monitors

Today, our monitors offer the latest in applications flexibility, operator convenience,and reliability. Advanced monitor design extends Chromalox monitoring capability beyond temperature to pressure, level, flow, displacement, vibration and more. Series 3000 6-Channel Process Alarm Monitor
The 3000 provides a complete continuous monitoring and instantaneous alarm system for bearing and stator applications. Years of trouble free operation in various other applications prove the 3000's reliability.
The 3000 monitor provides full-scale indication and alarm point readout for 6 individually adjustable alarm channels with independent alarm relay points.

Series 3111 Single Channel Temperature Monitor
The 3111 monitor is the first single channel alarm monitor that accepts RTD or thermocouple inputs with field selectable alarm parameters built into 1/4 DIN package.		 　


　
　 Model 3421 Digital 1/2 DIN Process Alarm Monitor
The ultimate flexibility in process alarms, with 8-channel monitoring, 2 alarms per channel. in one convenient, 1/2 DIN unit.

Accepts sensor inputs to monitor flow, level, PH, viscosity, vibration and more. Front panel pushbutton adjustments of all alarm functions, set points and calibration. Alarm relays can be assigned high or low, latching or non-latching, normally energized or de-energized, and disabled or enabled. Unique security feature prevents unauthorized programming.
