Chromalox electric comfort heaters provide efficient, economical heat for commercial and industrial applications. Three basic types of 　 heaters are forced air, radiant and convection, with combinations of these often used. They supply safe, clean, fast heat, and their heavy- 　 duty construction affords long, dependable service.

Radiant heaters are furnished in a wide variety of fixed as well as easily portable models.		 　
Forced air (blower) heaters can be
wall or ceiling mounted for greater
flexibility

　
　
Convection heaters come in a
wide variety of sizes for easy wall
mounting.
