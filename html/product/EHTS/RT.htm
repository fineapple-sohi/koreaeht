Round Tubular Heating Elements

KoreaEHT - Round Tubular heating KoreaEHT are manufactured according to the customer's requirements and are applied for the direct heating of various materials such as fuel oil, lubricating/hydraulic oils, waxes, organic solids, paraffine, water etc. The direct contact with the material guarantees an optimum heat-transfer.
The standard design of the round tubular heating elements consists of a 2" brass-nipple with soldered stainless steel flatpressed tubular heating elements and an attached connection box of Silumin in IP 34, if requested with integrated capillary-tube thermostat, single-pole, temperature ranges 12-82℃, 30-110℃, or 60-130℃.
Next to the standard-material stainless steel 1.4571, heating elements in copper or acidproof steel 1.4435 are available for certain applications.

Important
During operation, round tubular heating elements have to be completely covered by the fluid.
All heating elements are also available with special voltages, lengths as well as with different wattages.
