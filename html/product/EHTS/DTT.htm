Drum Heating Tapes

A simple and quick solution to keep the fluidity of media in drums or to heat them up. Besides the drum heating tapes for 200 | standard-drums, the eltherm-programme also includes standard-heaters for smaller barrels(see table).

Standard-Design
  • all types with thermostat
  • vulcanized silicone-insulation with glass-fibre
  • width of heating tape 75 mm
  • mounting with hook and spring
  • connection cable 1.5 m long without plug
  • all types with earth-connection
